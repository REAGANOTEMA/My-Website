About Amari Amman Foundation

Empowering Communities
Empowering vulnerable communities with hope, dignity, and sustainable support.
Providing education, healthcare, and advocacy for lasting change.
Together, we transform lives and build a brighter future.

Amari Amman Foundation is a humanitarian organization dedicated to restoring dignity and hope to vulnerable communities. Our work spans health outreach, education support, food security, and advocacy. We believe that every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, deserves an opportunity to thrive. Through partnerships, volunteerism, and love in action, we empower families and transform communities. With a focus on sustainable impact, we bring compassion where it’s needed most and stand committed to long-term change.

Our Management Team

Executive Director

The Executive Director oversees strategy, partnerships, and leadership. With vision and passion, they guide the Foundation toward impactful, sustainable service.

Programs Manager

Responsible for the planning and implementation of all community-based programs, ensuring measurable results and local empowerment through outreach.

Finance Officer

Manages the Foundation’s budgeting, reporting, and donor funds to ensure transparency, accountability, and proper resource allocation at every level.

Community Coordinator

Connects our field teams to local leaders, families, and schools ensuring smooth execution of services and identifying real-time community needs.

Communications Officer

Manages all communications, public relations, and media outreach to enhance the Foundation’s visibility and community engagement.

Volunteer Coordinator

Oversees recruitment, training, and support for volunteers, ensuring meaningful involvement and positive impact in all programs.
